Why Bamboo?

  • Fast Growth: Phyllostachys species are the fastest-growing woody plants—some can shoot up to 3 feet in a single day.
  • Carbon Champion: Absorbs CO₂ at twice the rate of many hardwoods, making it excellent for sustainable landscapes.
  • Natural Durability: Antimicrobial and pest-resistant—no need for harsh chemicals.
