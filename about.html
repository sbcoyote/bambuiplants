We're not just a business; we're a family of growers passionate about the power of bamboo.

Our Story

Bambuiplants started from a simple idea: what if we could create a privacy screen that was beautiful, fast-growing, and good for the planet? That journey led us to bamboo. We were captivated by its strength, versatility, and the serene environment it created in our own backyard here in Georgia.

What began as a personal project soon grew into a passion for sharing these incredible plants with others. We learned everything we could about cultivating the healthiest, most vigorous rhizomes, focusing on cold-hardy varieties that thrive in the American Southeast and beyond.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted source for fellow gardeners, landscapers, and DIY enthusiasts on Etsy and eBay. Every rhizome we ship is dug fresh from our own groves, packed with care, and sent with the hope that it will bring as much joy to your space as it has to ours.
